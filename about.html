The Beginning

In a world where people constantly struggle to find balance between work, health, relationships, and personal growth, Rasheda Khanom embarked on a mission—to help individuals create a truly balanced life.

Personal Transformation

Her journey began with personal transformation. As a teacher, entrepreneur, and lifestyle balance coach, she discovered that success is not just about achieving goals but about aligning every area of life—career, well-being, family, and self-fulfillment.

Developing the Approach

Inspired by the principles of BRAVE and years of practical experience, she developed a structured approach to sustainable life balance.

Birth of a Brand

With this vision, rashedak.com [Balance Life] was born—a brand dedicated to guiding people toward holistic success. Through coaching, education, and innovative programs, the brand empowers individuals to break free from stress, gain clarity, and design a life where every element thrives in harmony.

Global Impact

Expanding globally, the brand aims to transform lives across cultures, from young professionals to seasoned entrepreneurs, from students to parents. It is not just a business—it is a movement toward a healthier, more fulfilled world.

MEET THE FOUNDER

Rasheda Khanom

Teacher | Entrepreneur | Lifestyle Balance Coach

Rasheda Khanom is a passionate advocate for balanced living and holistic success. With a background in education and entrepreneurship, she brings a unique perspective to the field of personal and professional development.

Her journey began as a teacher, where she observed firsthand the struggles many people face in balancing their professional responsibilities with personal well-being. This insight, combined with her own experiences as an entrepreneur, led her to develop a structured approach to achieving sustainable life balance.

Today, through rashedak.com, she is fulfilling her mission of empowering individuals worldwide to create lives where career, health, relationships, and personal growth exist in harmony.

Rasheda's expertise spans multiple disciplines, including:

  • Lifestyle balance coaching
  • Personal and professional development
  • Stress management and mindfulness
  • Educational program development
  • Entrepreneurship and business strategy

Her methodology is grounded in the principles of BRAVE (Believe, Reflect, Act, Validate, Evolve) and draws from years of practical experience working with individuals from diverse backgrounds and life stages.

Rasheda believes that true success is not measured by achievements in a single area of life, but by the harmony and fulfillment experienced across all domains.

Her philosophy centers on these core principles:

  • Balance is achievable for everyone, regardless of circumstances
  • Sustainable change requires structured approaches, not quick fixes
  • Personal growth and professional success are complementary, not competing
  • Each individual's path to balance is unique and should be honored
